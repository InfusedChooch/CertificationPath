## 2. Professor Messer SY0-701 Study Notes (free, per-section PDFs)

**Target filenames:** `professor-messer-sy0-701-notes-[section].pdf`

**How to get it:**
1. Go to: https://www.professormesser.com/security-plus/sy0-701/
2. Open each video page — there is a free **"Course Notes"** PDF download link below the video
3. Download the notes for at least these high-priority sections:
   - Domain 4 (Security Operations) — heaviest exam weight at 28%
   - Domain 5 (Governance, Risk & Compliance) — your primary gap at 20%

4. Save each as `professor-messer-sy0-701-[domain]-notes.pdf`
